Idle Gilded Tycoon

Grow your Gilded empire, hire the working class, upgrade, and become a Captain of Industry!

About:

Idle Gilded Tycoon is a game about the Gilded Age period of American history. So, what exactly was the Gilded Age?

Timeframe:

The Gilded Age could be considered to have begun after the Civil War, (so late 1860's and onwards) and ended in the very early 1900's (when the Progressive Era began).

Description:

The Gilded Age got it's name from Mark Twain and Dudley Warner's novel named "The Gilded Age: A Tale of Today." The novels name highlighted the fact that the period was often, from an outside perspective, seen as prosperous and good, though on the inside was, well, not so great. The divide between rich and poor grew drastically, tenement living conditions for the poor became out of control, rights for labours were ignored despite growing Union movements, and the Government looked at the mess and did nothing at best since corruption in both local and federal government spread by the power of political machines.

Impact:

The Gilded Age's impact on American society was pretty large, as you would expect of a period that highlighted the good (and bad) of our society. Having this period, though, could be considered a more positive thing despite its base value issues, as, by revealing these issues, things could be fixed for the better. And this is exactly what happened with the coming of the Progressive Era, during which major social, political, and cultural changes occurred.
